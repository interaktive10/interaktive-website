INDUSTRIAL CASE STUDY

Revolutionizing Boyd's Product Visualization with Interactive 3D Solutions

Boyd, a world-leading innovator of Engineered materials and Integrated thermal management solutions, sought to revolutionize their product visualization by employing advanced 3D solutions. The objective was to provide their end users with an immersive and interactive experience to explore the intricate details, inner workings, and customizable features of their products, ultimately facilitating more informed decision-making.
Challenges:
Enable end users to interact with 3D models, view annotations, and play animations
Display the internal components and mechanisms through animations
Show high-quality exploded view and cross-sectional renders
Accurately represent the complex products in 3D and AR
Simulated animations with value statements
Over the past two years, we have had the pleasure of collaborating closely with our client, completing over 30 successful projects together.

We have delivered a diverse range of services to ensure that their needs are met, as highlighted below:
3D Product Configurator: Empowering Customers with Interactive Experiences
Exhibit 1: We created a 3D interactive widget for Boyd, showcasing a virtual smart city with distinct blocks representing each industry they cater to. Users can rotate the 3D model to explore individual industries and click on annotations to learn about Boyd's offerings. This responsive widget allows anyone to easily understand and visualize Boyd's diverse business landscape.
3D Modeling and Rendering: Capturing the Essence of Boyd's Advanced Solutions
We created photorealistic 3D models and renderings of Boyd's products to accurately represent their complex designs and innovative features. Our high-quality visualizations enabled Boyd to present their solutions more effectively to their customers, stakeholders, and marketing channels.
Animations: Revealing the Inner Workings of Boyd's Cutting-Edge Equipment
Our team developed dynamic animations, including exploded views and cross-sectional representations, to illustrate the internal mechanisms and components of Boyd's products. These animations helped Boyd demonstrate their engineering prowess and showcase the functionality and value of their solutions.
Augmented Reality: Bringing Boyd's Products to Life in Real-World Environments
We optimized and repurposed the 3D animation models for Augmented Reality (AR), allowing Boyd's clients to visualize their products within real-world environments at true-to-life scale.
Summary:
Our collaboration with Boyd led to the development of an engaging and interactive experience that showcased their cutting-edge product offerings. The combined use of 3D modeling, animations, product configurators, and augmented reality helped Boyd convey the intricate details and complexities of their equipment while empowering their customers to make more informed decisions. Our visualization solutions not only enhanced Boyd's marketing and sales efforts but also strengthened their reputation as a leading innovator in the thermal management and engineering industry.
