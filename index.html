FAQs
Why 3D models?

- 3D model of a product can be used for virtual photoshoots, animations, Augmented Reality, Virtual Reality, games, product configurators, etc..
- 6x cheaper than traditional photoshoot
- More shoppers are likely to purchase a product after interacting with the 3D model of a product

How do I place and track an order ?

Please provide us with some basic details, and we'll send you a payment link and delivery information. We believe in keeping our clients involved in every step of the process. We'll add you to our collaborative space where you can keep track of your order's progress.

What will the final order delivery contain ?

- 3D Model source file in GLB and USDZ formats (the best format for web, AR, and VR)
- Photorealistic ultra high-resolution renderings for virtual photoshoots
- For animation projects, output will be in Full HD (1920x1080px  resolution) in .mp4, or .mov formats.
- We will furnish you with the source files for product configurators that can be self-hosted. Additionally, we will provide an iFrame that can be embedded into your website using just one line of code.

All the files can be downloaded from the order page.

What other services do you provide?

We provide 3D product configuration service and 3D animation services as well. Let us know your requirements and we'll get back to you within 6-8 hours.

What if I am not happy with the work?

In the off-chance you’re not happy with the output, we offer unlimited revisions to make sure you get exactly what you’ve envisioned.

Can you sign NDA (Non disclosure agreement) ?

Yes, we even have our NDA format which you can use.
